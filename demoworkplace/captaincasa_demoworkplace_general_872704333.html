The context of this demo is the creation of a dialog using the CaptainCasa framework for the following purpose:

  • General
  • General Issues
  • Complex expressions - from 20251006 on

From 20251006 on the expression management was updated and now uses the "official" expression language support that is part of the javax.el/jakarta.el processing.

We still recommend to reduce the usage of complex expressions to some "healthy minimum" - it's just a good idea to separate the logic from the layout as stronly as possible...
