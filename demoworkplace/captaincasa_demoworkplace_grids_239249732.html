The context of this demo is the creation of a dialog using the CaptainCasa framework for the following purpose:

  • Grids & Trees
  • Grids and Trees
  • Grouping column headers
  • Grouped columns - "rendered" with columns

Click the check boxes and the corresponding columns will be rendered - or not rendered.

This demo shows that the grid column grouping can also used together with the normal "rendered" management of columns.
